Our School

Established in 1992 the Uyghur Language School has been a source of community and education for countless students.Established for the greater East Turkistan community the Uyghur Language School has sought to teach in an engaging way the language and customs of East Turkistan. The school participates in a number of cultural activities ie dancing, plays, sports; in order to unite people interested in East Turkistan culture and language.

Our Aims

We aim to develop an environment where children can learn with interest and at the same time develop a sense of belonging to Uyghur/Eastern Turkistani Culture.Foster a sense of identity among the people of East Turkistan work to sustain and develop our language and express and share our cultural heritage for the benefit of the wider Australian community.
